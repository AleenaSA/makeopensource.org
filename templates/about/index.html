About

MakeOpenSource is a collaborative open-source club led by University at Buffalo computer science students. Our club is dedicated to the development and deployment of open-source software. Our goal is to develop unique and intuitive ideas that will benefit the general public. All projects created under the club will be available to view, modify, and change given the restrictions of the individual project's license.

Team

MakeOpenSource is recognized by University at Buffalo's Department of Computer Science and Engineering as a full-time Computer Science club. Our team is comprised of undergraduate and graduate computer science students.
