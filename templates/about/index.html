About

MakeOpenSource is a collaborative open-source club led by computer science students at the University at Buffalo. Our club is dedicated to the development and deployment of open-source software. All projects created under the club will be available to view and modify given the restrictions of each individual project's license.

Our Team

MakeOpenSource is recognized by University at Buffalo's Department of Computer Science and Engineering as a full-time Computer Science club. Our team is comprised of undergraduate and graduate students from the University at Buffalo. Membership is open to all University at Buffalo students regardless of their background in computer science and open source.

Idealab

Idealab is a forum for new open-source project ideas. If you would like to submit an idea, please fill out the following form. Once the idea is approved it will appear on the IdeaLab webpage. Once ideas are approved and migrated, your idea will officially be a project and will appear in the Projects webpage.

Projects

All Projects visible on the MakeOpenSource Github page are open to contributions. Each project will have a contributions.txt file to specify a contribution format unique to that project, to ensure that submission adhere to existing formats.

Get Involved

Current University at Buffalo Students can join the club through our join portal. For updates on upcoming activities and events, join our Discord server.
