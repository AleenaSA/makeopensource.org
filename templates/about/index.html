About

MakeOpenSource is a collaborative open-source club led by University at Buffalo computer science students. Our club is dedicated to the development and deployment of open-source software. All projects created under the club will be available to view and modify given the restrictions of each individual project's license.

Team

MakeOpenSource is recognized by University at Buffalo's Department of Computer Science and Engineering as a full-time Computer Science club. Our team is comprised of undergraduate and graduate students from the University at Buffalo.
